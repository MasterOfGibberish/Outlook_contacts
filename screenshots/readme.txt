Place your Outlook Contact Exporter screenshots in this folder.

Required screenshot:
- outlook_tab.png - A screenshot of the "Contact Tools" tab in Outlook

You can take a screenshot by:
1. Open Outlook
2. Install the add-in
3. Look for the "Contact Tools" tab
4. Press Windows+Shift+S to take a screenshot
5. Save it as "outlook_tab.png" in this folder
